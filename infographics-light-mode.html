At a Glance

History of the M4 Sherman

Designed 1941 • First production 1942

Designed: 1941 to replace M3 Lee/Grant.

Production Begins: Feb 1942 — welded/cast hulls, 75 mm gun.

Scale: Over 49,000 produced by 1945.

First Combat Use

Late 1942 introduction

Debuted at El Alamein (Oct 1942) with the British. U.S. forces fielded it in Operation Torch (Nov 1942).
